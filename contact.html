Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent technical issues, please join our Discord community for faster assistance.

Do you offer custom model training?

While our primary focus is on our general-purpose models, we're open to discussing custom solutions for unique use cases. Please contact us with details about your project.

Can I contribute to TAI Research?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions to our open-source projects. Check out our GitHub repository for opportunities to contribute. You can also apply for our internship programs.

Do you offer educational discounts?

As students ourselves, we understand the importance of education. We offer special pricing for educational institutions and student projects. Contact us to learn more.

Built by Students, For Developers

We're a team of high school students passionate about AI. Despite limited resources, we've created TAI to make powerful AI models accessible to everyone. Your support helps us continue improving and expanding our services.
