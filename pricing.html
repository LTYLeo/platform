Frequently Asked Questions

How does token billing work?

Tokens are consumed based on the length of your input and output. We bill based on the total tokens used across all API calls in a billing cycle. Unused tokens do not roll over to the next month.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

All new accounts receive 5,000 free tokens to test our API. No credit card required to get started.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise plans we also support bank transfers.
