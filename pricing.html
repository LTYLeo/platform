Frequently Asked Questions

How does pay-as-you-go billing work?

You add credit to your account, and we deduct costs based on your actual usage. There are no monthly subscriptions or commitments. You only pay for the tokens you consume.

What's the difference between input and output tokens?

Input tokens are the text you send to our models. Output tokens are the text generated by our models. Output tokens typically cost more because generating text requires more computational resources.

Is there a free tier?

Yes! All new accounts receive 5,000 tokens completely free to test our API and build your first integration.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Alipay, WeChat Pay, and major credit cards. All transactions are processed securely.

How accurate is the token calculator?

The calculator provides estimates based on standard tokenization. Actual usage may vary slightly depending on your specific inputs and model parameters.
